Welcome to Isla Verginia Resort

Isla Virginia Resort has been a peaceful and exciting oceanfront destination since 2008. Nestled along the stunning coastline of Baler, Aurora, our resort spans one hectare of lush greenery, offering the perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Whether you're looking for a tranquil retreat or an action-packed getaway, Isla Virginia has something for everyone. Our well-appointed rooms and private villas provide comfort and serenity, while our refreshing swimming pools invite you to unwind under the tropical sun. Savor delicious local and international cuisine at our on-site restaurant, where every meal is crafted with fresh ingredients and a touch of coastal charm.

For thrill-seekers, Isla Virginia offers a variety of exciting water activities, including surfing and kayaking, . Explore the breathtaking beaches, ride the famous Baler waves, or take a scenic boat tour along the coast. Nature lovers can immerse themselves in the surrounding beauty with jungle hikes, waterfall excursions, and beachfront bonfires under the starlit sky.

Perfect for families, couples, and groups, Isla Virginia Resort is more than just a getaway—it’s a destination where unforgettable memories are made. Experience the warmth of Filipino hospitality, the beauty of the Pacific Ocean, and the adventure of Baler all in one place.

Escape. Explore. Experience Isla Virginia.
